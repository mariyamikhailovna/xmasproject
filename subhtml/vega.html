Admire Vega

(アドマイヤベガ)

VA: Hitomi Sasaki

The lone star that shines brightest. She carries the weight of her immense resolve.

  • Birthday: March 12
  • Height 157 cm

A somewhat shadowy Umamusume with no desire to get involved with the people around her. Something seems to have happened to her in her childhood, and her racing activities may be an attempt to atone for what happened. She's a stoic downer who shouldn't be left alone. She often gets caught up in the antics of her classmate T.M. Opera O.
